AllRize Leadership Team

Erik Ruda, Chief Executive Officer

Erik Ruda, CEO of Rize Technologies, Rizeware Solutions, AllRize and SunRize Marketing, is a distinguished tech executive with a software and IT consulting background spanning over two decades. He began as a Software Developer at Arthur Andersen and rose through the ranks at Vertex Inc. In 2012, he became the Principal Software Consultant at Vertex and significantly impacted its global software development status. In 2020, he founded Rize Technologies, showcasing his entrepreneurial spirit and drive for innovation.

Ruda holds an MBA from the University of Miami and has completed advanced studies in big data and AI at Johns Hopkins, combining deep industry knowledge with a commitment to technological advancement.

Adam Lowenstein, Chief Experience Officer

Adam Lowenstein is an experienced sales leader with cross-functional competencies in sales enablement, sales operations, territory management, business development, and CRM deployment. With a robust background spanning over two decades, Adam drives business development, account management, and sales leadership.

His career includes pivotal roles in both small enterprises and Fortune 100 companies, emphasizing employee growth, key account stewardship, and high-impact performance in technology sales. Adam also serves as the CXO at Rize Technologies, Rizeware Solutions and AllRize, where he continues to leverage his extensive expertise to foster growth and innovation.

Ali Yamani, Chief Operating Officer

Ali Yamani is a seasoned IT professional with over 25 years of experience as a CTO and COO. He began his career as a research scientist at Canon Research Center America, earning a patent at the age of 25. Ali holds a master’s degree in software engineering and has served as an ambassador of Architecture for a Silicon Valley-based e-Commerce software enterprise, where he was recognized with a star award for his innovative contributions. His career spans various senior roles, including Sr. Architect, Executive Director, CTO, and COO, during his extensive work in the Middle East.

Ali’s leadership and expertise have been instrumental in developing robust IT infrastructure and optimizing system procedures and operations for our companies. Today, Ali is the COO at AllRize, Rizeware Solutions, and Rize Technologies.

Mark uses his creativity around building talent models to drive consistent success, building high-performance work teams, and establishing retention-based cultures. Today, Mark helps guide the human resources strategy for Rize Technologies, Rizeware Solutions, AllRize, and SunRize Marketing.
